# SmartQ Flow

## Core Journey

1. A customer signs in and chooses a branch and banking service.
2. SmartQ calculates valid appointment slots from working hours, staff availability, service duration, and existing bookings.
3. The customer books, reschedules, or cancels an appointment.
4. On the service day, staff view the queue, call the next customer, and update the status.
5. Managers monitor branch performance through analytics and operational metrics.

## Roles

- `CUSTOMER`: Books appointments and tracks queue status
- `STAFF`: Manages active queues and service progress
- `ADMIN`: Manages branches, services, staff, and analytics

## Appointment Statuses

- `WAITING`
- `IN_PROGRESS`
- `COMPLETED`
- `CANCELLED`
- `NO_SHOW`
